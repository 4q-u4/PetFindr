Report a Lost Pet

If you've lost a pet, you can quickly and easily report it on PetFindr. Provide details about your pet, its last known location, and a photo. Our community and advanced algorithms will help in finding your pet.


Search for a Lost Pet

If you've found a lost pet, you can search for its owner on PetFindr. Enter details about the pet, such as its type and location. Our platform will help connect you with the pet's owner and facilitate a safe reunion.
